VIRTUAL MOUSE

About me

A virtual human computer interaction device that replaces the physical mouse or keyboard by using a webcam can be an alternative way for the touch screen.

How to use?

  • Use only one hand
  • When the camera detects your hand,slightly touch your thumb finger using index finger to move the cursor
  • To perform click operation,the circle around index finger should coincide with circle of thumb finger
